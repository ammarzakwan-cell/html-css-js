    History of Babbacombe Model Village

    Babbacombe Model Village is a miniature village and railway located
    in Babbacombe in Torquay, Devon. The model village was opened
    in 1963 by Tom Dobbins The site is set on approximately 4 acres (1.6 ha).
    There are over 400 models along with 1,000 ft (300 m) of model train
    track in the village. The model village is primarily one town, an entirely
    fictional one, but many buildings within them are based on UK prototypes
